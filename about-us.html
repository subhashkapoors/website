About Us

Studentnest.com was the creation of Chander & Monika Joshi, who in helping family members realized that there was no "centralized" area where these services could be obtained. Studentnest.com is being created with the needs for all kept in mind such as: elementary; high school; & college students as well as their parents and teachers.

Everything will be done in our power to ensure that this site stays safe for everyone involved. We are also parents of young children and like any other parent, want our children to surf sites which are safe and educational.

With this in mind, we will continue to grow this site with your assistance and hope that you make this "your" site as well.
